Dominique Terry Design System



This Design System will be used for my first professional portfolio. The goal of this portfolio is to present my work in a clean, fresh way that feels both familiar and unique.

Within this mini site are the Foundations, Components, and Patterns that make up the portfolio.

Foundations

Within the Foundations tab you will find the typographical hierarchy of the portfolio as well as the brand colors and logo iterations that will be used.

Components

Here you'll find the components that add style and flair to the portfolio, including the header, button, and case study cards.

Patterns

The pattern section includes the templates of page layouts on the site. Here you will see two different page patterns at three different sizes to accommodate desktop, tablet, and phone screens.
