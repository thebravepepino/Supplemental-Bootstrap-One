UX Case Study Cards

Below is an example of the case study cards that will be used on the UX Case Study page.


Usage

These cards need to convey the goals and subject matter in as few words as possible.


Accessibility Considerations

To help distinguish between the case studies, each study will have a signature color that will be shown on the card and incorporated throughout the actual case study.


Example Case Study Card

Case Study Title

This is the summary of the case study. It will be kept short to only 2 or 3 sentences - just enough to give potential viewers the essence of the project.
