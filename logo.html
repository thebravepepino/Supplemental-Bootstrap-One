The Objective


My objective was to create a logo that portrays a brand of quality, innovation and a sense of playfulness. I wanted to keep the image simple and informative, while playing around with typeface and color.


Aspects that Meet the Objective:


  1. The sans-serif that was used, Jiyukoba, has clean, strong lines and the letters all have equal weight. This gives a very modern, expensive feeling to the logo.
  2. Originally I thought I would use three colors, but keeping the color scheme to black and white or green, it allows the “U” and “I” to stand out from the “N.”
  3. The bright green pop of color nods at the playfulness in the objective.
